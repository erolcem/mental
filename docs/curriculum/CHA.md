## 4. khmer — Khmer 🇰🇭
**Goal:** "Fluency Where No Textbook Ecosystem Exists"

**Factual fix: Khmer has NO lexical tones** (the old tree said "Subscript
Consonants & Tones") — its difficulty is the script's two consonant series,
subscripts, and vowel-length/aspiration contrasts. No standardized public
exam exists, so verification is ILR-style self-assessment + native-rated
recordings. Aakanee/SEAlang are the canonical free resources.
